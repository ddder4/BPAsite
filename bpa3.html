Overview:

Video Games not only have a positive use, but they also under certain circumstances can also have negative effects. Video games if used without moderation have harmful effects on a person's health. Some people who have current health issues, might experiance greater side effects when playing Video Games.

Unhealthy Games:

Some games that are bad for people to play are:
  • Civilization V
  • Minecraft
  • League of Legends
The same games that can produce a positive side effects can also produce negative side effects. For instance, someone who has anger issues can play a game like minecraft to relieve stress where another person with anger issues can play the same game and die inside the game and then become enraged. Playing video games for an extended period of time can develop things like Carpal Tunnel and poor hygiene. They can also develop eating disorder because they put the game before their own personal good.

Negative Side Effects:

Health:

People's health is a big concern when they are addicted to video games. When addicted to games people will ignore many things that are important to their health. One thing that people do when addicted to video games is stop eating and starve themselves. The reason why is because they think their games are more important than making sure that they are well nourished. Also people do not take care of their personal hygiene. People will go for long periods of time without cleansing themselves.
