Overview:

Most parents that dont know much about video games think that they only kill brain cells and are bad for kids to play. This is not necessarily true. Video games actually provide people a lot of skill that they can use in real life! Some of these skills can be aplied better than others, but they are all still helpful.

Healthy Games:

Some games that are good for people to play are:
  • Civilization V
  • Minecraft
  • League of Legends
Team based games like League of Legends make the player work with a team and communicate to accomplish a goal. A strategy game like Civilization V make a player use their head and think of a way out of the situation they are currently facing. A free open world game like Minecraft make players use their creative side to build whatever they want and they feel accomplished when they achieve their goal.

Positive Side Effects:

Some games are better to play than others. Strategy games make you use your head and think of different ways to outthink your opponent. First person shooter games build multi-tasking skills because the player must focus and keep track of different things going on all at once. A third thing that is a positive outcome from playing games is teamwork skills. In most competitive games such as League of Legends, the player works on a team of other people to push and fight to the enemy base. The teams work together and push toward the other team's spawn point and cover each other in order to defeat the other team. A fourth thing that playing video games helps with is communication. Comunication and Teamwork go hand in hand. When a person plays games with a team, a crucial part of teamwork is communication. When the player plays the game they need to talk to their team in order to make things known. They ask for help and notify a teammate of an enemy's position and then they work together in order to fight that enemy.
